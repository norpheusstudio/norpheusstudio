Games

REPUBLIKA

Genre: FPS

"Republika" is an immersive Filipino FPS experience and immersed in a battlefield set in the picturesque and challenging terrain of the Philippines.
"Republika" apart is its unique trading system, which adds an exciting layer to the gameplay. As players navigate the game's world, they can engage in trading to acquire and customize weaponry, enhancing their chances of success in intense firefights, all while exploring the stunning and diverse landscapes of the Philippines.
The game's dedication to creating an authentic and adrenaline-pumping gaming experience makes it a must-play for both FPS enthusiasts and those who appreciate the rich culture and unique landscapes of the Philippines.

ESKINITA

Genre: HORROR/SURVIVAL

In a chilling horror story set in the eerie alleys of a forgotten town, countless horrifying creatures lurk in the shadows, waiting to unleash their terror on unsuspecting victims. As night falls, the narrow, dimly lit alleys become a nightmarish playground for a variety of malevolent beings, from ghastly spirits to grotesque monsters. The protagonists must navigate this terrifying labyrinth of darkness and face their deepest fears to survive the night.
